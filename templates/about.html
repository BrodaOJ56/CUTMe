About CUTMe

CUTMe is a simple and efficient URL shortening tool designed to make your URLs as short as possible. In today's fast-paced world, it's important to keep things concise, and that's where CUTMe comes in. Whether you're sharing links on social media, sending URLs via text message, or simply want to tidy up long web addresses, CUTMe has got you covered.

With CUTMe, you can quickly shorten your URLs and obtain a compact, easy-to-share link. Additionally, CUTMe provides QR codes for your shortened URLs, allowing for seamless scanning and sharing on various platforms. Say goodbye to lengthy, unwieldy URLs and hello to streamlined, user-friendly links with CUTMe.

To start using CUTMe, simply log in or create an account. Authentication is required to ensure the security and privacy of your shortened URLs. If you already have an account, you can log in here to access the URL shortening and QR code generation features.
